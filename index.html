The Top 25 College Football Stadiums with the Largest Capacity

The SEC and Big Ten Dominate When it Comes to Stadium Capacity

This map shows the Universities with the largest football stadiums in terms of capacity. The SEC and Big Ten take up over half the list with 9 and 7 stadiums respectively. The University of Michigan tops the list with a max capacity of 107,601. Click on the markers to get the name of the University, the name of the stadium, the city the stadium is in, and its max capacity. All of the data and more information can be found on the NCAA's website.
